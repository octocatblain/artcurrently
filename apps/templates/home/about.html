Art Currently Digital Magazine

Art Currently is an online publication that explores contemporary art culture. We cover all things art - from exhibitions, people, trends and events - to bring you into the ever-changing art world, and into the minds of those who are shaping it.

What we do

We are everything Art & Culture.

Art Currently is a dedicated platform for designers, writers, editors, and art collectors. Every artist we collaborate with joins the team and together, we overcome obstacles and rejoice in accomplishments.

The Art Currently team is engaged in the art community, always curious about what's next in art, and generally fun to be around. We have a culture of collaboration and a roster of talent.
